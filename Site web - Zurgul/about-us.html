What is Aurora Foundation?

Aurora Foundation is an independant organization first
made in Paris, we have now a global presence. Our main
goal is to spread
the word and teach about the dangers of climate change.
Our long term goal is to use our presence and knowledge
to change our current energy ressources to reusable
and ecological energy source.

We are always recruiting new members
and we do global events around ecology in which we debate
about the current state of our planet and what could be done
to eradicate pollution.
